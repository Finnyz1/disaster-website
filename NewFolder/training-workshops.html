Training & Workshops

Learn how to prepare, respond, and recover from emergencies with expert-led training sessions and hands-on workshops.

Why Attend Disaster Preparedness Training?

Being prepared can save lives. Our workshops and training programs provide essential skills such as first aid, emergency planning, and survival techniques.

Available Training Programs

  • First Aid & CPR Certification: Learn life-saving techniques for treating injuries in emergencies.
  • Emergency Response Team Training: Become a trained volunteer for disaster response efforts.
  • Fire Safety & Evacuation Drills: Understand how to prevent and respond to fires.
  • Basic Survival Skills Workshop: Learn how to secure food, water, and shelter in disasters.
  • Family & Community Preparedness: Develop a custom emergency plan for your household and neighborhood.

Disaster Preparedness
