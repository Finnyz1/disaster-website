Disaster Preparation Strategies

Being prepared for disasters can save lives. Follow these essential strategies to stay safe and ready.

Personal Preparedness

Prepare yourself and your family by staying informed, having emergency contacts, and knowing what to do in different situations.

  • Stay updated on weather and emergency alerts.
  • Have a personal emergency contact list.
  • Practice emergency evacuation drills.

Home Safety

Ensure your home is disaster-ready by securing structures and keeping emergency supplies available.

  • Secure heavy furniture to walls.
  • Store emergency supplies such as food, water, and medicine.
  • Have fire extinguishers and first-aid kits ready.

Emergency Planning

Plan for emergencies by identifying evacuation routes and establishing a family emergency plan.

  • Know the nearest evacuation centers.
  • Have a meeting point in case of separation.
  • Assign responsibilities to family members.

Community Preparedness

Get involved in your community’s disaster response efforts and participate in training programs.

  • Join local disaster preparedness programs.
  • Be aware of community emergency response plans.
  • Volunteer for disaster response training.
