Earthquake Preparedness

üåç Earthquake Preparedness: What to Do Before the Next Big One

The Philippines sits on the Pacific Ring of Fire, making it prone to earthquakes. Follow these key steps:

  • Before: Secure heavy furniture, prepare a family emergency plan, and join earthquake drills.
  • During: Duck, Cover, and Hold under sturdy furniture. Stay away from windows.
  • After: Expect aftershocks, check for gas leaks, and evacuate if necessary.

Building an Emergency Kit

üéí Building the Ultimate Go-Bag: Essentials for Every Filipino Household

When disaster strikes, a **Go-Bag** can save lives. Make sure yours includes:

  • Drinking water (3-day supply)
  • Canned food & energy bars
  • Flashlights, extra batteries, and power banks
  • First aid kit and essential medicines
  • Whistle and emergency contacts
  • Cash, IDs, and important documents in a waterproof case
